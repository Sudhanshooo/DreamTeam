  • The day I couldn't deny anymore

    February, 2021

    The DLF day!! Oh, how can i forget that day, I was just mesmerised and lost all my senses the time I looked into her eyes that day every little moment of that day is just so crystal clear in my head even now. To all the fears she might have, WE WILL FIGHT THEM TOGETHER, and that's how we took first step to this journey. OUR JOURNEY.
